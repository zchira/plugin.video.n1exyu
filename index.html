About

This is Kodi plugin for N1-Info regional tv station. N1-Info covers ex-Yu region (Serbia, Croatia and BiH). It allows you to watch live streams for any of three regions.

This isn't official plugin and I am not in any connection with TV N1. I made it for my personal use, because my cable tv operator doesn't have N1-Info channel in any packets.
